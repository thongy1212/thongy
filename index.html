All about Sir Isles

Your Lordship is an intelligent, fine young man. He may not seem much, however, he is our 'Jewllery of Hope'. His head glistens in the sun, illuminating the area, shining onto trees, soldiers, and also rocks. His head is like a beacon, shining into the sky, warning everyone of Your Lordship's arrival. He resembles, a true king.
